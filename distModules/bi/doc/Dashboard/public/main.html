    • Text: A plugin for loading text resources.

    • Material Design: Adding a Material Design look and feel to websites.

    • Highstock-export-csv: The exporting module allows users to download the chart as csv document.

    • Moment: Used for parsing, validating, manipulating, and displaying dates in JavaScript.
    • DateTimePicker: Date selector used for the Date Filter.
